ABOUT DPSIMUN 2018

CRAS ES NOSTER

It gives us immense pleasure to play host to the third edition of  Delhi Public School Indirapuram Model United Nations, after its  successful launch in the year 2016. Like the previous editions of DPSI MUN, this conference would continue to be a platform to fashion opinions, to ignite ideas and promote international peace and understanding. We believe that  wars, no matter what reason they are fought for, are the greatest  threat to the present-day human race. Violence acts as a precursor to more conflicts, rather than solving existing ones. If at all something can put an end to it, it is the change in the mindset of  people, especially our youth, and what can be a better way to do it than by stimulating and empowering the young! With this thought in mind, we embark upon the third edition of DPSI MUN. With young diplomats debating and deliberating various issues of vital importance, we are sure it will be a great learning experience to cherish forever.

UNGA

"Diplomacy is the art of telling people to go to hell in such a way that they ask for directions."
-Winston Churchill
The United Nations General Assembly continues to be the chief representative and deliberative policy-making organ of the United Nations. Abiding by its 'One Member,One Vote' convention, it is a forum that urges it's nations to strengthen multilateral relations and in the course of their dialogue, prioritize preventing the conflicts of tomorrow. Please note that the UNGA is a Double Delegation​ committee.
